What Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Your name (as you enter it)
  • Email address (via Google Sign-In or direct signup)
  • Your target exam selection (UPSC, SSC, Banking, RBI)

Usage Data

To personalise your experience and show you your progress, we store:

  • Quiz attempt history — scores, accuracy, time taken
  • Your current streak and streak shield count
  • XP points and level
  • AI question bank balance
  • Number of MCQs generated

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your phone number, location, device identifiers, contacts, camera access, microphone access, or any biometric data.
🔒

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Run your personalised quiz experience
  • Track your preparation progress over time
  • Show you your performance on the leaderboard
  • Provide AI-powered explanations via our chat feature
  • Send you streak and progress notifications (if enabled)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

🤝

Third-Party Services

Micro Mock uses the following trusted third-party services to function:

Supabase

Our database and authentication provider. Your account data and quiz history are stored securely on Supabase servers. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant. Supabase Privacy Policy →

Google Sign-In

Used for account creation and login. We only receive your name and email from Google — nothing else. Google Privacy Policy →

Groq AI

Powers our AI chat feature. Your questions to the AI are sent to Groq for processing. We do not store your chat history beyond your current session. Groq Privacy Policy →

DiceBear

Generates your profile avatar. Only a hashed, anonymous seed value is sent — your user ID is never exposed. DiceBear Privacy Policy →

Razorpay

Processes Pro subscription payments. We never store your payment card details — all payment data is handled by Razorpay directly. Razorpay Privacy Policy →

📊

Leaderboard & Public Data

If you complete a daily quiz, your name and score may appear on the public leaderboard visible to other Micro Mock users. This is limited to:

  • Your display name (as entered during onboarding)
  • Your quiz score and accuracy for that day
  • Your target exam
Your email address is never shown publicly anywhere in the app.
🗑️

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can request:

  • Data export — a copy of everything we hold about you
  • Data deletion — complete removal of your account and all associated data
  • Data correction — update your name or exam target at any time in the app

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@micro-mock.in. We will respond within 7 business days.

🔐

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication uses industry-standard PKCE OAuth flow
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • XP and score modifications go through server-side RPC functions — not directly from the client
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) is enforced in our database — users can only access their own data
👶

Children's Privacy

Micro Mock is designed for adults preparing for government competitive exams. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Free Plan & Pro Subscription

Free Plan

The free plan includes access to daily quizzes, basic AI chat (3 questions/day with rollover), PYQ practice, and basic streak tracking.

Pro Subscription

Pro is a monthly subscription at ₹149/month, billed through Razorpay. Pro includes:

  • 4 Streak Shields per month
  • Full Subject Diagnosis
  • Weekly Prep Report
  • Unlimited AI questions
  • Unlimited MCQ generation

Free Trial

New users receive a 7-day Pro trial automatically. No payment is required to start the trial. After the trial ends, the account reverts to the free plan unless upgraded.

Refunds

Subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing period has begun. If you believe you were charged in error, contact us within 48 hours at support@micro-mock.in.
